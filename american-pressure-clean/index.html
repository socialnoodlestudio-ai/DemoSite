§ 01 SERVICES INVENTORY

What we clean — and how we clean it.

Six categories of work. Different surfaces want different pressure, different detergent, different technique. We match the method to the material — no one-size blast jobs.

  1. 01

    Driveways & Walkways

    Oil, rust, tire marks and embedded dirt lifted from concrete, pavers and brick. Edges and joints included.

    CODE DW-01 PSI 3000–4000
  2. 02

    Roof Soft Washing

    Low-pressure treatment to clear black streaks, algae and mildew without damaging shingles or tile.

    CODE RF-02 PSI < 500 · SOFT
  3. 03

    House & Exterior Walls

    Stucco, vinyl siding, painted block — restored without stripping paint or driving water under panels.

    CODE HX-03 PSI 1500–2500
  4. 04

    Patios, Pool Decks & Pavers

    Bring back the color of travertine, marble and pavers. Sealing available on request.

    CODE PV-04 PSI 2000–3000
  5. 05

    Fences & Wood

    Wood fences, gates and decks brightened without splintering. Restoration over replacement.

    CODE FW-05 PSI 1000–1500
  6. 06

    Commercial & Multi-Unit

    HOAs, plazas, restaurants and rentals. Scheduled visits keep entryways and dumpster areas presentable.

    CODE CM-06 FREQ WK / MO
