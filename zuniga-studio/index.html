[ 02 / Manifesto ] A small studio that ships like a serious one

Small businesses deserve a serious studio.

Most of the web in 2026 is templated. Fast to ship, identical to the next one over. Beautiful in screenshots and forgotten by Friday.

We do the opposite. Every Zuniga site is hand-built — strategy, brand, code, motion — to the same restraint, the same intention a luxury studio would apply to a Fortune 500. The difference is who we apply it to: ambitious Florida small businesses that have outgrown templates.

We work with three clients at a time. We answer email ourselves. And we ship sites you can show off without apologizing.

[ 04 / Approach ] Four phases · One studio · Personally led
  1. I

    Discovery

    A 90-minute working session — in person where possible, on video when not. We map your business, audience, and the moments that matter most. You leave with a written strategy doc inside 5 days.

    Deliverable · Strategy document + sitemap
  2. II

    Strategy

    Content architecture, brand language, and the first wireframes. We commit to the structure before anyone touches color — strong bones before pretty paint.

    Deliverable · Wireframes + content scaffold
  3. III

    Design & Build

    Visual design and hand-coded build run in parallel. You see the site in your browser within two weeks — not a Figma mock, the real thing — and we iterate from there.

    Deliverable · Living site at a preview URL
  4. IV

    Launch & Tend

    We deploy, we set up the local-SEO foundation, and we don't disappear. A three-month tending window is included with every build — small adjustments, content changes, analytics review.

    Deliverable · Live site + 3-month tending window
