[ The Studio ] Founded 2026 · Greenacres, Florida

A small studio for businesses that want a site that holds its weight.

Zuniga Studio is a boutique digital design practice based in Greenacres, Florida, founded by Clarisa Zuniga. We work with three clients at a time and ship sites you can show off without apology.

— Founder · Clarisa Zuniga

I left a bigger studio so I could give every project my full attention.

I spent the early years of my career inside larger studios, which taught me what good process looks like — and what happens when there's too much of it. Talented people pulled across ten things, every meeting a status check, every client somewhere in the queue.

Zuniga Studio is what I wanted instead. Three clients at a time. Personal attention through every phase. The same restraint you'd get from a luxury consultancy, applied to the businesses that actually need it — the ambitious Florida shops, boutiques, and practices that have outgrown templates.

If you're considering a project, write to me directly. I read every inquiry myself and respond inside 24 hours.

Clarisa Founder · Designer · Sole Engineer · Greenacres, Florida

[ The Approach ] Four phases · One studio · Personally led
  1. I

    Discovery — 1 week

    A 90-minute working session — in person where possible, on video when not. We map your business, audience, and the moments that matter most. You leave with a written strategy doc inside 5 days.

    Deliverable · Strategy document + sitemap
  2. II

    Strategy — 2 weeks

    Content architecture, brand language, and the first wireframes. We commit to the structure before anyone touches color — strong bones before pretty paint.

    Deliverable · Wireframes + content scaffold
  3. III

    Design & Build — 6–8 weeks

    Visual design and hand-coded build run in parallel. You see the site in your browser within two weeks — not a Figma mock, the real thing — and we iterate from there.

    Deliverable · Living site at a preview URL
  4. IV

    Launch & Tend — ongoing

    We deploy, we set up the local-SEO foundation, and we don't disappear. A three-month tending window is included with every build — small adjustments, content changes, analytics review.

    Deliverable · Live site + 3-month tending window
