FAQ

Questions, answered.

How much does a website cost?+

Most small business sites land between $1,200 and $3,500 depending on page count, features (like online booking), and copywriting needs. We always quote a flat price up front — no hourly surprises.

How long does it take?+

Average turnaround is 14 days from approval. Simple sites can be live in a week; larger projects with custom photography or e-commerce take a bit longer.

Do you really do a free preview?+

Yes. You give us a few details about your business and we send back a real homepage preview within a few days. If you love it, we keep going. If not, you walk away with zero owed and zero hard feelings.

Can I update the site myself after launch?+

Absolutely. We build on platforms that are simple to update, and we include a short training session so you can change text, photos, and prices without calling us.

Do you only work with Florida businesses?+

Most of our clients are in Palm Beach and across Florida, but we work with small businesses anywhere in the US.
