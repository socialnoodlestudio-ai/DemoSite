Common Questions

What people ask before booking.

How long does a full wrap take?+

Most full color changes run 3–5 days depending on the vehicle and how much disassembly is involved. We'll lock in a window when you book and keep you posted day by day.

How dark can I legally tint in Florida?+

Florida law allows 28% VLT on front side windows, 15% on rear side and rear windows, and a non-reflective strip on the windshield above the AS-1 line. We'll walk you through what's legal and what looks best on your specific glass.

What's the warranty?+

Workmanship is guaranteed for multiple years — if anything lifts, peels, or bubbles from install, come back and we fix it. Material warranties depend on the specific vinyl or film; premium brands carry 5–10 year coverage.

Is wrapping safe for my factory paint?+

Yes, on factory paint in good condition. Quality vinyl protects the paint underneath and removes cleanly when done right. We'll inspect before quoting and flag any spots that might be a concern.

Do you do commercial fleet work?+

Absolutely — partial wraps, logo install, full fleet color changes. Bring us the vehicle list and we'll put together a package.

Can I bring my own vinyl?+

We only install premium materials we can warranty — 3M, Avery Dennison, KPMF, Inozetek, XPEL. If you have a specific film in mind, ask us first and we'll source it.
